Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to provide?
You'll need to provide your logo (if you have one), text content for each page, images you want to use, and your contact details. I'll send you a simple checklist after booking.
Is hosting included?
Hosting is not included in the package price. Basic hosting typically costs £3-10 per month depending on your needs. I can recommend affordable hosting providers or help set it up for you.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes! Package deal sites are a great starting point. As your business grows, we can add features, pages, or even rebuild with a fully custom solution.
What kind of SEO is included?
All packages include on-page SEO: optimised page titles, meta descriptions, heading structure, image alt tags & google console integration and local keywords. Higher packages include Advanced SEO that include competitor analysis and keyword research tailored to your business.
Do you offer support after my website is complete?
Yes! All projects include training if needed and post-launch support to help you get started. Ongoing maintenance is available at an arranged fee if required.
