Frequently Asked Questions...

How long does a typical web development project take?
Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most websites take 4-12 weeks from initial consultation to launch. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation. Unless you are choosing a package deal, then it could take as little as a few days.
Do you offer support after my website is complete?
Yes! All projects include training if needed and post-launch support to help you get started. Ongoing maintenance is available at an arranged fee if required.
What's included in your SEO services?
We offer set SEO services to boost your search rankings and drive organic traffic, which available as add-ons or standalone packages. From audits and basic fixes to full optimization, each option is designed to suit your goals and budget. See our packages for more info.
Can you work with my existing website?
Absolutely! We can redesign, optimize, or add new features to existing websites. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach for your goals and budget.
Do you offer payment plans?
Although we don't offer payment plans, we don't require full payment upfront. Instead, we offer milestone based payments to make larger projects more manageable. A 25% deposit is required to begin work and secure your project in our schedule, covering the initial consultation, planning, and mockup phase. The remaining balance is divided across agreed upon project milestones, ensuring you're comfortable with progress at each stage before proceeding. Full payment terms are available in our terms and conditions.
