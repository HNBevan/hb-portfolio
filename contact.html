Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does a typical web development project take?
Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most websites take 4-12 weeks from initial consultation to launch. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
Do you offer support after my website is complete?
Yes! All projects include training if needed and post-launch support to help you get started. Ongoing maintenance is available at an arranged fee if required.
What's included in your SEO services?
Our SEO services include keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO audits, content strategy, link building, and monthly reporting to improve your search engine rankings.
Can you work with my existing website?
Absolutely! We can redesign, optimize, or add new features to existing websites. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach for your goals and budget.
Do you offer payment plans?
No, however we offer package deals that bundle popular features together at a discounted rate. These are designed to be affordable and provide great value for small businesses. For larger custom builds, we offer milestone based payments to help spread the cost. Contact us to discuss the best option for your project and budget.
