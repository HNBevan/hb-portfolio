What is SEO & Why Does It Matter?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of improving your website so it appears higher in Google search results. When someone searches for services you offer, you want your business to appear on the first page, not buried on page 10 where no one looks.

For local Kent businesses, good SEO means appearing when nearby customers search for your services. Whether someone searches "plumber in Ashford" or "web designer Canterbury," proper optimization helps you get found by the people who are actively looking for what you offer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to work?
SEO is a long-term strategy. You'll typically start seeing noticeable improvements in 3-6 months, with continued growth over time. Factors like competition, your current website state, and industry affect the timeline.
Can you guarantee first page rankings?
No reputable SEO professional can guarantee specific rankings, as Google's algorithm is constantly changing and rankings depend on many factors. I focus on proven best practices that consistently improve visibility over time.
Do I need SEO if I have a new website?
Yes! New websites especially benefit from SEO. Without optimization, your site may never appear in search results. Starting SEO early helps you build visibility from the beginning rather than trying to catch up later.
What's the difference between SEO and paid ads?
Paid ads (Google Ads) give immediate visibility but stop when you stop paying. SEO builds organic visibility that lasts. Learn more about how SEO works. Most businesses benefit from both, but SEO provides better long-term value.
Can I start with a one-off service and then get ongoing support later?
Absolutely. Plenty of clients start with an audit or local SEO setup to get the foundations right, then decide they want ongoing support once they see the value. There's no pressure either way. Just get in touch and we'll take it from there.
